Turtles, are members of the reptile group Testudines,they are captivating animals with a distinct physical form characterized by a protective shell that doubles as their portable abode. These remarkable creatures have existed for millions of years and have successfully acclimated to diverse ecosystems such as freshwater, saltwater, and land-based habitats.
