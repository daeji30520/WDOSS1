Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery
The main objectives of the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery are to protect sea turtle eggs, provide a safe environment for hatching, and release the hatchlings into the sea. Additionally, the hatchery aims to raise awareness about the importance of sea turtle conservation among the local community and visitors.

Conservation
Efforts

The hatchery is dedicated to conserving sea turtles through various initiatives, including collecting and protecting turtle eggs, caring for injured turtles, and raising awareness about the importance of turtle conservation.

Release
Programs

Once the hatchlings are ready, the hatchery organizes regular turtle releases, allowing visitors to witness the amazing sight of the tiny turtles making their way into the ocean. These releases help ensure the survival and growth of the turtle population.

Education and Awareness

The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery actively promotes educational programs and awareness campaigns to highlight the threats faced by sea turtles due to human activities and the importance of their conservation. They aim to inspire visitors and local communities to take part in protecting these magnificent creatures.

About Us

We are dedicated to the conservation of turtles and their natural habitats. Our mission is to raise awareness, promote research, and support initiatives that protect these incredible creatures.
